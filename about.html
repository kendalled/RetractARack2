Features, Specs, & F.A.Q

Features

The Retract-a-Rack is a modern, overhead storage solution for your outdoor gear. Its retractable design locks into place for easy loading and retracts back to the ceiling automatically, stowing all your items securely. Organize a variety of products from all-seasons sporting goods to garden tools, and create more space in your garage or workshop. In a few simple steps using basic hand tools, Retract-a-Rack mounts to the ceiling of your garage or work shop and organizes your gear for good! Equipped with pneumatic gas shocks, it lowers and retracts back into place effortlessly. Stop wasting time organizing and get out there!

Specifications & Dimensions

  • Weight: 45 pounds | Length: 91.5 in | Width: 39 in
  • 100% Durable Metal Construction
  • Weatherproof Coating
  • Holds 30+ pounds of gear
